PK Image Gallery

PK Image Gallery is a web app showcasing 100 stunning images across 10 categories. Explore high-quality photos with a clean and user-friendly interface.

Features
	•	100 beautiful images
	•	10 organized categories
	•	Responsive and fast-loading
	•	Easy navigation

Categories

Nature | Animals | Architecture | Technology | Travel | Food | Art | Fashion | Sports | Miscellaneous

How to Use
	1.Download the project or clone it from GitHub:git clone https://github.com/pranesk19-ux/pk-image-gallery.git
        2.Open index.html in your browser.
	3.Browse images by category and click any image to view full size.
